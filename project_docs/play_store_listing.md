## Full Description (Under 4000 characters)
Welcome to NoteMeFy, the privacy-first notes and reminders app that remembers what you need to do—and exactly **where** you need to do it.

Ever walked into a grocery store and instantly forgot what you needed to buy? Or arrived at the office only to realize you forgot to ask your coworker an important question? With NoteMeFy, those moments are a thing of the past. 

Simply write a note, attach a location, and let NoteMeFy do the heavy lifting in the background. As soon as you enter your geofenced area, you'll receive a push notification with your note exactly when it matters most.

**KEY FEATURES:**

**Geofenced Reminders**
Stop relying on time-based alarms. Attach real-world locations to your notes and to-do lists. Get notified automatically the second you arrive at or leave a specific place.

**True Offline-First Privacy**
Your data is yours. NoteMeFy is built with a local-first vault architecture using secure on-device storage. No cloud servers, no data harvesting, and no internet connection required for your core notes to function.

**Lightning Fast & Distraction-Free**
Built natively using Flutter for a buttery-smooth experience. Jot down your thoughts with a gorgeous, premium interface designed to get out of your way so you can focus on what matters.

NoteMeFy is the ultimate companion for your daily commute, errands, and spontaneous ideas. Download NoteMeFy today and let your location trigger your memory!
