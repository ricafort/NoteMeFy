# Privacy Policy for NoteMeFy

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at NoteMeFy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us.

This privacy policy applies to all information collected through our application, **NoteMeFy**, and any related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Location Data
The NoteMeFy app utilizes location services, including **Background Location Data**, to enable "Geofenced Notes". 
- **Foreground Location:** Used to set locations for note reminders when the app is open.
- **Background Location:** We request background location access to trigger push notifications when you enter or leave a defined geofence, even when the app is closed or not in use.
*All location data is processed locally on your device for the purpose of triggering these reminders. We do not transmit, track, or store your continuously updated location on any external servers.*

### B. User-Generated Content
We collect the notes, text, and other content you create within the app.
- **Local Storage:** All your notes and related content are stored securely and locally on your device using local encrypted databases. We do not transmit or read your personal notes. 

### C. Financial Information
We offer in-app purchases and subscriptions. All transactions are securely processed by the Google Play Store and our third-party provider, RevenueCat. We do not directly collect, process, or store your credit card or billing details.
- [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect or receive to:
- **Deliver Notifications and Geofenced Reminders:** We use your device's background location to check against your saved note-triggers and deliver a local notification.
- **Provide and Manage the Service:** To operate and maintain the features within NoteMeFy.
- **Manage User Purchases:** To manage user accounts and subscription status via RevenueCat.

## 3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share information with the following third parties to operate our service:
- **RevenueCat:** For processing subscriptions and in-app purchases.

## 4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?
Since NoteMeFy relies on an offline-first architecture, your note data and specific geofence locations are kept indefinitely on your local device until you choose to delete the note, clear the app data, or uninstall the app.

## 5. Security of Your Information
We aim to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures, including local device encryption. 

## 6. Access and Data Deletion
Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete individual notes or your entire data cache directly from the app's settings or by uninstalling the application.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have questions or comments about this notice, please reach out to the developer contact email associated with our Google Play Store listing.
